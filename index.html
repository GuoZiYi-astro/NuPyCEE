Welcome to the NUPYCEE website

NUPYCEE is the chemical evolution platform of the

here NuGrid collaboration. The user can choose between the Stellar Yields for galactic modeling applications (SYGMA) module and the One-zone model for the evolution of galaxies (OMEGA) module. SYGMA allows to follow the ejecta of of simple stellar populations in a closed box while OMEGA deals with the simulation of galaxies with inflows and outflows.
